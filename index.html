Our Certifications & Quality Standards

Meeting European Union import requirements and international quality standards

🌿

Phytosanitary Certificate

CRITICAL FOR EU

Official government-issued certificate confirming products are free from quarantine pests and meet EU plant health regulations.

  • Issued by Nigerian Plant Quarantine Service (NPQS)
  • Required for ALL agricultural exports to EU
  • Valid for 14 days from issue date
  • Includes inspection report and treatment records
✓ Available for all shipments
🔍

SGS Inspection & Verification

CRITICAL FOR EU

Independent third-party inspection by SGS (Société Générale de Surveillance) at point of loading.

  • Quality and quantity verification
  • Weight and packaging inspection
  • Loading supervision
  • Clean Report of Findings (CRF)
✓ Arranged per buyer request
♻️

Organic Certification (EU Organic)

PREMIUM OPTION

EU Organic Regulation (EC) No 834/2007 compliant certification for organic products.

  • Certified by EU-accredited bodies (e.g., Ecocert, Control Union)
  • Available for: Hibiscus, Sesame, Shea Butter, Ginger, Cashew
  • Full traceability from farm to export
  • Annual inspection and audit
✓ Available on request (lead time: 3-6 months)
🛡️

HACCP

Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points food safety management system.

  • Systematic preventive approach to food safety
  • Identifies physical, chemical, biological hazards
  • Required by many EU food processors
✓ Implemented across supply chain
📋

ISO 22000:2018

International food safety management system certification.

  • Combines HACCP principles with management system elements
  • Global recognition by food manufacturers
  • Continuous improvement framework
→ In progress (Expected Q3 2026)
🌱

Global G.A.P

Good Agricultural Practice certification for farm-level production.

  • Food safety and sustainability standards
  • Traceability of produce
  • Environmentally friendly farming
  • Worker welfare compliance
✓ Available for selected farms
🤝

Fair Trade (FLO)

Fairtrade International certification for ethical sourcing.

  • Fair prices for farmers
  • Community development premiums
  • Environmental standards
  • Strong appeal to European ethical buyers
✓ Available for Shea Butter, Cashew, Cocoa
🧪

Aflatoxin Testing (B1, B2, G1, G2)

CRITICAL FOR NUTS

Laboratory testing for aflatoxin contamination in nuts and dried fruits.

  • EU limit: 4-5 ppb for cashews, 2-8 ppb for other nuts
  • Tested by accredited laboratories (e.g., SGS, Bureau Veritas)
  • Certificate of Analysis (COA) provided
  • Pre-shipment and loading testing
✓ Mandatory for all nut shipments
📜

Certificate of Origin (CoO)

Official document certifying goods originate from Nigeria.

  • Issued by Nigerian Chamber of Commerce
  • Required for customs clearance in EU
  • Supports preferential trade agreements
✓ Included with all shipments
🇪🇺

EUR.1 Movement Certificate

Enables preferential tariff rates under EU-ACP Economic Partnership Agreement.

  • Reduces or eliminates import duties
  • Proof of originating status
  • Valid for ACP countries including Nigeria
✓ Available for qualifying products
