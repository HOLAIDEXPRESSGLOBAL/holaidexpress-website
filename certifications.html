Our Certifications & Quality Standards

Meeting European Union import requirements and international quality standards

At Holaidexpress Global Biz Nig. Ltd, we understand that European buyers demand the highest standards of quality and compliance. Our comprehensive certification portfolio ensures that every shipment meets EU regulations and international food safety standards.

CRITICAL FOR EU

Phytosanitary Certificate

Official government-issued certificate confirming products are free from quarantine pests and meet EU plant health regulations (EU 2016/2031).

Key Details:

  • Issued by Nigerian Plant Quarantine Service (NPQS)
  • Required for ALL agricultural exports to EU
  • Valid for 14 days from issue date
  • Includes inspection report and treatment records
  • Compliant with EU Plant Health Law
Available for all shipments
CRITICAL FOR EU

SGS Inspection & Verification

Independent third-party inspection by SGS (Société Générale de Surveillance) at point of loading, ensuring quality and quantity verification.

Inspection Services:

  • Quality and quantity verification
  • Weight and packaging inspection
  • Loading supervision and sealing
  • Clean Report of Findings (CRF)
  • Certificate of Inspection
Arranged per buyer request
PREMIUM OPTION

Organic Certification (EU Organic)

EU Organic Regulation (EC) No 834/2007 and 2018/848 compliant certification for organic agricultural products.

Certification Scope:

  • Certified by EU-accredited bodies (Ecocert, Control Union)
  • Available for: Hibiscus, Sesame, Shea Butter, Ginger, Cashew
  • Full traceability from farm to export
  • Annual inspection and audit
  • EU organic logo eligibility
Available on request (lead time: 3-6 months)

HACCP Certification

Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points food safety management system ensuring systematic preventive approach to food safety.

Food Safety Management:

  • Identifies physical, chemical, biological hazards
  • Critical control points monitoring
  • Required by many EU food processors
  • Codex Alimentarius compliant
Implemented across supply chain

ISO 22000:2018

International food safety management system certification combining HACCP principles with management system elements.

International Standards:

  • Global recognition by food manufacturers
  • Continuous improvement framework
  • Interactive communication
  • System management approach
In progress (Expected Q3 2026)

Global G.A.P

Good Agricultural Practice certification for farm-level production ensuring food safety and sustainability.

Sustainability Standards:

  • Food safety and sustainability
  • Traceability of produce
  • Environmentally friendly farming
  • Worker welfare compliance
Available for selected farms

Fair Trade (FLO)

Fairtrade International certification for ethical sourcing ensuring fair prices and community development.

Ethical Sourcing:

  • Fair prices for farmers
  • Community development premiums
  • Environmental standards
  • Strong appeal to European ethical buyers
Available for Shea Butter, Cashew, Cocoa
CRITICAL FOR NUTS

Aflatoxin Testing

Laboratory testing for aflatoxin contamination (B1, B2, G1, G2) in nuts and dried fruits. Critical for EU compliance.

Testing Standards:

  • EU limit: 4-5 ppb for cashews, 2-8 ppb for other nuts
  • Tested by accredited labs (SGS, Bureau Veritas)
  • Certificate of Analysis (COA) provided
  • Pre-shipment and loading testing
  • EU Regulation (EC) No 1881/2006 compliant
Mandatory for all nut shipments

Certificate of Origin (CoO)

Official document certifying goods originate from Nigeria, required for customs clearance in EU.

Documentation:

  • Issued by Nigerian Chamber of Commerce
  • Required for customs clearance in EU
  • Supports preferential trade agreements
  • ICC (International Chamber of Commerce) format
Included with all shipments

EUR.1 Movement Certificate

Enables preferential tariff rates under EU-ACP Economic Partnership Agreement (EPA).

Trade Benefits:

  • Reduces or eliminates import duties
  • Proof of originating status
  • Valid for ACP countries including Nigeria
  • EU-ACP EPA compliant
Available for qualifying products
