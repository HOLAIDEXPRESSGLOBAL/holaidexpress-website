About Holaidexpress

Bridging Nigerian Agriculture with European Markets

Our Story

Holaidexpress Global Biz Nig. Ltd was founded with a clear mission: to connect the rich agricultural heritage of Nigeria with the quality-conscious markets of Europe. What started as a small trading operation in Lagos has grown into a trusted export company with offices in Nigeria and France.

We work directly with farmers across Nigeria's key agricultural regions - from the cocoa plantations of Ondo to the sesame fields of Nasarawa, from the shea butter cooperatives in Niger to the hibiscus farms of Kano. This direct connection ensures fair prices for farmers and premium quality for our European buyers.

Our name reflects our philosophy: "Holaid" represents our holistic approach to sustainable trade, while "Express" signifies our commitment to efficient, reliable delivery. We believe that agricultural export should benefit everyone in the value chain - from the farmer in the field to the consumer in Europe.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver certified Nigerian agricultural products to European markets with complete traceability, fair farmer compensation, and uncompromising quality standards—backed by phytosanitary compliance and SGS verification.

👁️ Our Vision

To be the preferred Nigerian agro export partner for European buyers, known for reliability, transparency, and sustainable trade practices that empower local farming communities.
